27th July — Your Birthday

My Dearest Ali,

On this beautiful day, as the world celebrates your birthday, I find myself lost in gratitude — grateful that you exist, grateful that our paths crossed, and grateful that I get to love someone as extraordinary as you.

You came into my life like a quiet miracle — not with grand gestures, but with small, sincere moments that slowly became the most important parts of my day. The way you check on me, the way you laugh, the way you make even ordinary moments feel like something worth remembering — that is your magic, Ali. And it is irreplaceable.

I want you to know that you are not just someone I love — you are someone I deeply admire. Your patience, your kindness, your strength in silence — these things move me in ways I can never fully put into words. You carry your heart with such grace, and you have no idea how beautiful that is to witness.

Thank you for every moment you chose me. Thank you for every time you listened when I needed to be heard. Thank you for staying, even when it was hard. Thank you for loving me the way you do — purely, genuinely, and without condition.

I pray to Allah that He fills your life with every joy your heart quietly longs for. May He bless you with peace that surpasses understanding, with health that lets you chase your dreams, and with a future so bright and beautiful that it takes your breath away.

I see a future with you — one that is warm and full of laughter, full of small adventures and quiet evenings, full of growth and gratitude and love that deepens with every passing year. I want to be there for every version of you. The happy one, the tired one, the dreaming one. All of you.

Today is your day. Let it remind you of how deeply, endlessly, and completely you are loved.

With every beat of my heart —

Happy Birthday My Love ❤️

Always Be With Me.

— Alishba 💕
